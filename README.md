# Sales-Performance-Dashboard
Sales Analysis using Python and Power BI
## 🛠️ Tools Used
Python | Pandas | Matplotlib | Seaborn | Power BI | DAX | Jupyter Notebook

## 📌 Project Overview
End-to-end sales analysis on 9,994 retail transactions spanning 4 years (2014–2017) 
with $2.3M in total revenue across 3 product categories and 4 regions.

## 📊 Dashboard Preview
![Sales Dashboard](salesdashboard1803.png)

## 🔍 Key Findings
- Technology category drives 36% of total revenue ($836K)
- Overall profit margin is 12.47% across 4 years
- Discounts above 20% consistently result in losses
- West region is the most profitable region
- Consumer segment contributes 50.56% of total sales
- Tables and Bookcases are biggest loss-making sub-categories

## 💡 Recommendations
- Cap discounts at 20% to protect profit margins
- Focus marketing budget on Technology category
- Investigate Central region low profitability
- Target Consumer segment with loyalty programs
- Stock inventory in Q3 for Q4 holiday season peak
